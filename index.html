Frequently Asked Questions

You'll need: PAN Card, Valid ID (Passport/Driving License/Aadhar), Address Proof (Utility Bill/Lease Agreement), Last 3 months bank statements, and proof of income (Salary slip/ITR).

The approval process typically takes 5-7 working days. You'll receive a call from our team for verification, and once approved, your card will be delivered within 10-15 days.

The minimum annual income requirement is ₹3 Lakhs for ESSENTIAL Card and ₹5 Lakhs for premium cards. However, for Business Cards, the requirement may vary based on business turnover.

No hidden charges. All fees are clearly mentioned and transparent. We charge only the annual fee (if applicable), GST, and statutory charges as per RBI guidelines.

You can track your reward points through our mobile app, website, or by calling our customer service. Points are updated in real-time and can be redeemed anytime.

We offer an interest-free period of up to 50 days (depending on the billing cycle) for purchases made during the billing month. Withdraw from any ATM also comes with an interest rate of 2.75% per month.
